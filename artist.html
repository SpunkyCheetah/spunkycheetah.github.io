Hello, I'm Loki (they/them or any pronouns), the person who made this website and all the fanart on it!

I am queer dual-enrollment college and high school technical program student who grew up homeschooled. I am studying video game development, programming, and art, as well as taking a web design class currently (hence this website!). I spend a lot of my free-time with fandom related stuff, specifically MCYT at the moment, and one of my main hobbies is drawing.

I have been making fanart for as long as I can remember, having spent a lot of my childhood making up OC creatues for Pokemon and Lilo & Stitch, or making comics and other art for Avatar: The Last Airbender, My Little Pony, Ninjago, and basically any other show, game, or book I knew of.

I also have a Minecraft sona/OC that I use for my own Minecraft-related playing and role-playing: c!Loki.

Their design varies somewhat across different things I use them for, but the basic idea generally carries over.
